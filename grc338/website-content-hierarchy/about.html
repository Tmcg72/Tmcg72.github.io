About Us

Akira Ramen is a family restaurant owned and operated by longtime residents of the Bay Area. We use the freshest ingredients to elevate our longtime family recipes to the next level. We offer a mix of traditional and trendy Japanese cuisine as part of our dining experience. We also offer a selection of japanese beverages, including soft drinks and a number of alcoholic options.

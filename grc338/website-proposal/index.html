Introduction

Sweet Tooth Waffles

We are obsessed with soft, buttery belgian waffles. We especially love the sweeter side of things and hope to give a window into our wonderful world of waffles! Choose from any of our signature waffles or top of your own waffle with a wide selection of toppings to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Target audience

This site would be browsed by potential new and returning customers when they are craving something sweet.

This site would allow them to view all of the different signature waffles as well as all of the toppings available to choose from. This webiste would also allow them to order ahead of time online to pick up in store.
